        Agent Corps

        Each agent has one job, one output, and one proof trail.

        IntakeTurns ambiguous email into structured requirements.
        PlannerBuilds the task graph and chooses the tools.
        MemoryRetrieves customer preferences and approved policies.
        CatalogChecks product availability, pricing, stock, and lead time.
        RiskBlocks unsafe discounts, low margins, and missing data.
        WriterGenerates the quote, rationale, and customer response.
        LearningConverts human edits into memory and policy proposals.
