        Agent operating model

        A coordinated business system, not a single chatbot.

        Hive Corps separates the quote workflow into focused agents with clear responsibilities, evidence, and handoffs.

        Intake AgentReads messy requests and extracts customer, quantity, urgency, and missing data.
        Planner AgentCreates the quote-to-response plan and chooses the right business tools.
        Memory AgentRecalls customer preferences, approved policies, and prior decision patterns.
        Catalog AgentChecks products, stock, pricing, delivery timing, and support options.
        Risk AgentReviews margin, discount policy, delivery promises, and approval requirements.
        Quote Writer AgentProduces customer-ready quote language and internal rationale.
        Learning AgentTurns human edits into proposed memory and policy improvements.
